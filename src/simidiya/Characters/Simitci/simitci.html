#Planning and Inspirations

Firstly, I'm not sure if my technical skills are sufficient for this character because I want to create a fully detailed character, from modeling to animation.

It's crucial for me to find an instrumentalist that won't stand out too much.

The violin-like instrument at the Incesaz concert I attended yesterday seems like the most logical choice. Moving the bow back and forth and hitting the right chords in a small area seems easier and more doable than a guitar. Also, the sitting posture while playing this instrument seemed the most original to me compared to others.

Or maybe placing the music sheet right in front of my hand to obstruct the view might be a better option. Also, this didn't seem like laziness but rather a display of cleverness to me.

Violin or stringed instrument

Saxophone with beats

When immersed in music, instead of taking the metro, start dancing

A song synchronized with movements

On November 30, 2023, after a day's break, I decided to make the character a saxophonist. I believe it will be more in sync with the energy of the stage. Plus, adjusting this sound for the project will be easier. I'm not sure how to convey what's in my mind about the guitarist on a guitar, but I can easily convey it humming to the saxophonist. If I can't achieve the sound with artificial intelligence, I'm thinking of collaborating with a saxophonist from Fiverr and basing the entire animation on this sound. These are my current thoughts.
